# Product Overview

## Peña Linda Bungalows Payment Processing System

This project manages payment transaction data and sales analysis for Peña Linda Bungalows, a hospitality business in Peru. The system processes and analyzes payment data from multiple payment gateways including:

- **Izipay** - Primary payment processor for online bookings
- **Culqi** - Secondary payment processor with POS terminal integration
- **Direct transfers** - Bank transfers and other payment methods

## Business Context

- **Industry**: Hospitality/Tourism (Bungalow rentals)
- **Location**: Peru
- **Currency**: PEN (Peruvian Sol)
- **Payment Methods**: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Yape, bank transfers
- **Customer Base**: Primarily Peruvian customers with some international guests

## Key Features

- Transaction data consolidation from multiple payment processors
- Sales reporting and analysis
- Commission calculation and reconciliation
- Payment status tracking and fraud analysis
- Multi-currency support (primarily PEN)
